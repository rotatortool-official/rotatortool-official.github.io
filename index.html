⬡ About Rotator
A free, real-time crypto rotation screener built for traders who want an edge.
Rotator was built with one goal: help everyday traders make smarter decisions about when to rotate their capital between crypto assets.

Instead of guessing which coin to buy next, Rotator scores every coin across 7, 14, and 30-day timeframes, then identifies which coins in your portfolio are overperforming (candidates to sell) and which coins in the market are lagging (potential rotation targets).

The tool is 100% free, no account required, and runs entirely in your browser. Your holdings are stored locally — we never see your data.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about Rotator.
What is Rotator?
Rotator is a free real-time crypto screener that tracks 200 coins including stablecoins with DeFi yield APR. It scores each coin across multiple timeframes and helps you identify rotation opportunities — when to move capital from outperforming coins into lagging ones that may be set to recover.
What does the Score mean?
The Score (0–100) is a composite rank combining 7-day, 14-day, and 30-day performance relative to all tracked coins. A score of 80+ means the coin is among the top performers. A score below 30 means it has been significantly underperforming — which may signal a rotation opportunity.
What is a "rotation"?
A rotation is when you move capital from a coin that has already had a strong run (and may be overbought) into a coin that has been lagging but still has solid fundamentals. The idea is to buy relative weakness and sell relative strength within a bull trend.
Is this financial advice?
No. Rotator is strictly an informational tool. All scores and signals are based on historical price data. Past performance never guarantees future results. Always do your own research (DYOR) and consult a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.
How do I unlock Pro?
Pro is completely free — no payment needed. Share your personal referral link with 3 friends. When each friend opens your link and adds at least one holding, Pro unlocks automatically. Click ⚡ UNLOCK PRO to get your link.
Is my data safe? Do you store it?
Your holdings and preferences are stored only in your own browser (localStorage). We never collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There are no accounts, no logins, and no servers receiving your portfolio information.
Where does the price data come from?
Rotator uses the CoinGecko public API as its primary data source, with CryptoCompare as a fallback. Data is fetched live when you load or refresh the page. There is no real-time streaming — hit Refresh to get the latest data.
What's coming next?
Forex pairs rotation tracker and stock portfolio screener are next on the roadmap. The goal is to make Rotator a unified dashboard for tracking all your assets — crypto, forex, and stocks — in one place.
